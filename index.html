India

  • The Indian Standard Time (IST) is based on a longitude of 82.5°, which passes through Mirzapur, near Allahabad in Uttar Pradesh.
  • It is 5 hours 30 minutes ahead of Greenwich Mean Time (GMT), now called the Universal Coordinated Time (UTC).

United States

  • Time in the United States, by law, is divided into nine standard time zones .
  • Standard time zones in the United States and other regions are currently defined at the federal level by law 15 USC §260.
  • This time is according to Los Angeles.

China

  • The time in China follows a single standard time offset of UTC+08:00, even though China spans almost five geographical time zones
  • The official national standard time is called Beijing Time domestically and China Standard Time (CST) internationally
